ABOUT ME

By playing with computers from a very young age I discovered a natural talent for utilizing technology. I learned my first programming language, C#, when I was 10 and wanting to build my own web browser.

Grew up in Baton Rouge, Louisiana and moved to Indianapolis, Indiana when I was 14 with no intention of pursuing computer science. After taking two semesters of web design in high school I immediately knew what I wanted to do after college. After learning HTML and CSS I took two AP Computer Science courses and learned my next two languages: Python and Java.

I am now a student at Purdue University pursuing a major in Web Design and Programming and hopefully a minor in Computer Science.
